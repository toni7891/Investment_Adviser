Mechanics

  • ⊟ HIDE button appears in the right side of each section's header bar
  • Click hides content with a 200ms ease-out transition (max-height → 0)
  • Section collapses to a rounded pill button with ▶ + section name
  • Click the pill → section expands back with 200ms ease-in transition
  • Both sections toggle independently
  • State resets to expanded on every page load (no localStorage)
